    A fast, free, distraction-free notepad that lives in your browser. No account, no sync, no cloud — just you and your notes.

    • Auto-saves everything as you type
    • Light & dark theme
    • Works offline — no internet needed

    Built with vanilla HTML, CSS & JavaScript. Notes stored in localStorage.
